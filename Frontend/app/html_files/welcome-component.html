ABOUT MILESTONES

There is a lot of ongoing and existing research on human achievement, and consequently, many theories about the best strategies for how to be successful and reach meaningful goals. If you search Google for how to achieve goals, you will find an endless lists of articles, blogs, and other material.

Usually, a critical missing piece that makes goal achievement difficult, is good planning. This includes considering things like the resources that will be needed, other people involved in the process, and forseeable obstacles that will come up along the way.

Another common oversight is the planning of small steps along the path to a final goal. Setting and planning for these Milestones can make a significant impact on your ability to stay the course and stay on top of all of the small habits and actions that will be needed to reach your ultimate goal.

Milestones helps improve the process of goal planning by designing the planning process around the smaller steps to be reached along the way to the final goal. As Aristotle said, we are what we repeatedly do. Excellence therefore is not the result of a single act, but of many acts that all add up to a final result.
